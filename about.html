History Overview

MinWares is a small business established by Masa Seumako on 31st-05-2020 in Gaborone. The business specializes more on selling different kinds of shoes. The company orders shoes from companies like Addidas, Nike and Ellesse to resell them. There is also a section or part in the business where they sell clothes, most of those clothes caps, T-Shirts(polo) and trousers. Minah Kicks orders its wears from different countries like China and the United Kingdom and they take some time to arrive in Botswana. Since Minah Kicks is a small company it is not well known it has problems with advertising and getting customers. Minah Kicks sells its products in the shop and interacts with more people when they are buying shoes. It resells products Missions Values Vision
